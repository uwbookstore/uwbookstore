Our Commitment to Accessibility

The University Book Store is committed to providing a website that is accessible and user friendly for all visitors. We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are continually working to improve the experience for everyone who uses our site.

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of our website or encounter content or functionality that may not be fully accessible, please email us at info@uwbookstore.com with "Website Accessibility" in the subject line. Please include a brief description of the issue or any suggestions for improvement, and our team will review your feedback promptly and work to address the concern.

If you need assistance using our website, placing an order, or obtaining information about our products and services, please call us at (608) 257-3784 during regular business hours.

We value your feedback as we work to create an inclusive experience for all customers.

While we do not control the accessibility of third-party websites, applications, or content providers that may be linked from our site, we encourage our partners to provide accessible and user-friendly experiences for all users.
