Wisconsin has a growing percentage of grade school students who are in need of financial assistance. Over the past decade the number of students who qualify for aid has increased by nearly 14%. In large school districts, over half of the students are low income. School is challenging enough for all students, without also missing basic necessities such as pencils and notebooks.

That is where we are stepping in. We designed a notebook to help students as more than just a writing tool. For each of the Notebook Project notebooks sold in our stores, we will donate one notebook to a Wisconsin student in need. Please join us in helping ALL students be equally prepared for their education!

The Notebook Project notebook will be available at all our store locations or order it online today!

Thank you for your purchases! We have already been able to donate 864 notebooks to the Odyssey Project and the University of Wisconsin Division of Diversity, Equity & Educational Achievement.
