Thank you for your order!
 
 
Our order processing time is 5-7 business days (Monday thru Friday) for ground shipping, 1-2 business days for expedited orders. Your order will be shipped via UPS®, FedEx®, or USPS®.
 
The processing time for most pickup at store orders is 1 to 2 days. You will receive notification when your order is ready for pick up.

If you selected pickup at our Brookfield location, please allow for a processing time of 4-8 business days.
 
Payment Processing
 
   

If you are using one (or more) University Book Store Gift Cards, Our system does check with your credit/debit card provider to make sure that there are sufficient funds available to cover the total amount of the order (your financial institution might list this fund check with a “Pending” notation). That happens even if you have entered a gift card(s) with a balance that covers the entire cost of the order. When your order is processed our system will apply the gift card(s) first. If that covers the total amount of the order your credit/debit card will not be billed anything. If the gift card covers a portion of the order total your credit/debit card will be billed the difference. Only if the gift card has no balance would the entire amount of the order be billed to your credit/debit card.
