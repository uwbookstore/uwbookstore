Book Signing with Mike Leckrone

Join us on Saturday, Nov. 23rd, from 2–4 PM at the University Book Store on State Street for an unforgettable book signing experience!

Meet former UW Director of Bands Mike Leckrone as he signs his captivating book, Moments of Happiness: A Wisconsin Band Story.

Discover the incredible stories behind 50 years of iconic performances, heartfelt dedication, and the passion that shaped the UW–Madison bands into legends.

Grab your copy, get it signed, and celebrate Badger pride with one of UW’s most inspiring figures. Copies of Moments of Happiness will be on-hand for purchase.

BONUS! After the event, join Mike Leckrone at the Shine on Madison Tree Lighting Ceremony in Downtown Madison and keep the celebration going!

SPECIAL GUEST: UW–Madison’s beloved mascot, Bucky Badger, will join the festivities from 2:00–3:00 p.m., adding extra fun and Badger pride to this exciting event!
