Mike Leckrone Returns for Second Book Signing Event

After the overwhelming success of his first book signing, Mike Leckrone, beloved former director of the University of Wisconsin Marching Band, is coming back for an encore!

Join us for this special event at the University Book Store in Hilldale Shopping Center on Saturday, December 14, from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

Known for his aerial stunts, dazzling sequined outfits, and the creation of the unforgettable Fifth Quarter celebration, Leckrone’s career was marked by breaking barriers and spreading joy. He opened doors for women to march in the band, welcomed special-needs musicians to perform at Camp Randall, and captivated audiences nationwide with his showmanship and passion. His book, Moments of Happiness: A Wisconsin Band Story, captures the heart and soul of his journey, offering fans a closer look at the man behind the music.

Don’t miss this opportunity to meet a Badger legend, celebrate his incredible legacy, and grab a signed copy of Moments of Happiness. Whether you're a lifelong fan or discovering his story for the first time, this event promises to be an unforgettable experience.

Copies of Moments of Happiness will be on hand for purchase.
