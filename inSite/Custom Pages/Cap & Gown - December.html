May 18 2023
Academic attire return

May 2023 Grads – still have your attire? Please return it as soon as possible.

Academic Attire returns may be done during regular store hours at any University Book Store location.

Bachelors – return gown, keep cap and tassel.
Faculty, Doctors and Masters – return gown and hood, keep cap and tassel.

Last day to purchase academic attire
Last day to purchase academic attire to arrive in time for the December 2023 ceremony. Rentals continue through Dec 16.
Nov 20–Dec 16
Academic attire rental continues (regular pricing applies)

Rent online (Nov 20 thru 12 midnight Dec 14) or in person (Nov 20 thru Dec 16) with fulfillment at the Lower Level Service counter (711 State St.), or over the telephone (1-800-993-2665 ext.5907) (add $10.00 for service fee) for the December 2023 commencement ceremony.

DEC 17 @ 2 P.M.
All online orders not picked up at the Campus Store by 2 p.m. Saturday Dec 17 will be transported to the Kohl Center for pick up there on Sunday morning.
Hilldale, HSLC, and Brookfield orders will remain at those stores.
