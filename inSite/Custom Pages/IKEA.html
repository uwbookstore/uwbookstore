Bring IKEA closer to home with free
local pick-up at the University Book Store.

Order online & pick up at the University Book Store on State Street. Pickup is often available the next day! For inspiration, visit our in-store IKEA display featuring Badger-inspired home & dorm room ideas.

How to Place Your Free IKEA Local Pick-Up Order:

  • Join IKEA Family for free to receive free local pick-up on orders over $35
  • Place your order at IKEA.us, in the IKEA app.
  • At checkout, choose your pick-up date and select the University Book Store as your location

After you check out, you will receive the following emails:

  • A confirmation email after purchase
  • A second email when your order is being prepared, including store pick-up hours
  • A final email when your order is officially ready

When you arrive at the University Book Store for your IKEA order, please check in at the Service Desk on the lower level

Please note there is no IKEA-specific parking. IKEA pickup hours are M-Th: 10:00 a.m. – 5:30 p.m., Fr-Sa: 10:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m., & Su: noon – 4:30 p.m. Come inside and check in at the lower level before loading. Loading assistance is not always available, so please arrive prepared and have your vehicle ready. Large orders may use our loading dock, but you must check in before parking behind the University Book Store. You can find up-to-date downtown parking availability here.
