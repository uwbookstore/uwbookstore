Congratulations, University of Wisconsin-Madison Graduates!

December 2023 Timeline

May 18 2023
Academic attire return

May 2023 Grads – still have your attire? Please return it as soon as possible.

Academic Attire returns may be done during regular store hours at any University Book Store location.

Bachelors – return gown, keep cap and tassel.
Faculty, Doctors and Masters – return gown and hood, keep cap and tassel.

Cap & Gown rental reservations for December 2023 will start on September 18th.

SEPT 18–NOV 10
Academic attire rental – save $20.00, early Badger pricing
Rent online (Sept 18 thru Nov 10) or in person (Sept 18 thru Nov 10) with fulfillment at the Lower Level Service counter (711 State St.), or over the telephone (add $10.00 for service fee) for the December 2023 commencement ceremony.
Oct 13
Last day to purchase academic attire
Last day to purchase academic attire to arrive in time for the December 2023 ceremony. Rentals continue through Dec 16.
Nov 11–Dec 16
Academic attire rental continues (regular pricing applies)

Rent online (Nov 11 thru Dec 11) or in person (Nov 11 thru Dec 16) with fulfillment at the Lower Level Service counter (711 State St.), or over the telephone (800-993-2665 ext.5907) (add $10.00 for service fee) for the December 2023 commencement ceremony.

DEC 16
Online orders must be picked up by the close of business Saturday (Friday for the HSLC) at your designated pickup location.

Dec 17
UW Winter Commencement

Return attire after use. All returns must be made by 7 p.m. Dec 19.
Dec 19
Academic Attire Returns

Academic Attire returns may be done during regular store hours at any University Book Store location. All returns must be completed by 7:00pm Tuesday Dec 19.

Bachelors – return gown, keep cap and tassel.
Faculty, Doctors and Masters – return gown and hood, keep cap and tassel.

Returns not completed by 7:00 pm Dec 19 will incur a $20 service fee.

May 2024 Commencement
Rentals will begin February 5, 2024
