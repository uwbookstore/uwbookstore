Moments of Happiness

Don’t Miss This Exciting Event!

Join us on Thursday, Nov. 23rd, from 2–4 PM at the University Book Store on State Street for an unforgettable book signing experience!

Meet legendary UW Director of Bands Mike Leckrone and acclaimed author Doug Moe as they unveil their captivating new book, Moments of Happiness: A Wisconsin Band Story.

Discover the incredible stories behind 50 years of iconic performances, heartfelt dedication, and the passion that shaped the UW–Madison bands into legends.

Grab your copy, get it signed, and celebrate Badger pride with two of UW’s most inspiring storytellers.

Bonus! After the event, join Mike Leckrone at the Shine on Madison Tree Lighting Ceremony in Downtown Madison and keep the celebration going! See you there!
