Since May 1, 2009, The University Book Store, at its 711 State Street location, has offered full service, in-house, custom framing. Staffed by John Sheean (B.S., M.A., and M.F.A. in studio art) with over 35 years of professional framing experience he can offer expert consultation on all framing projects. His framing department client base includes the Wisconsin Historical Society, the Wisconsin Historical Foundation, dozens of university departments, faculty, and students. He also provides services for many campus and local businesses, along with many local professional artists.

The services offered by John and his framing department include custom mat cutting, picture glass cut to order, as well as custom assembled metal and wood frames. Custom framing of award certificates and other framing needs for UW Department offices are an emphasis of John’s framing services. Small orders for mat and glass cutting services can generally be provided within 48-72 hours. Most other custom frame orders require a 10-14 day turn around, but rush services are available.

In addition to the above John’s framing career has completed projects for the governor's and various state official's offices at the State Capitol, projects for the UW Athletic Department, and along with the dozens of UW-Madison Departmental offices previously mentioned. All framing questions, problems, and projects are always welcome for consult no matter how large or small.
