What is it?

The University Book Store is proud to partner with Second Harvest and NBC15 to help our neighbors facing food insecurity. The goal is to raise 5.5 million meals!

How do I donate?

Visit any of our Madison locations to drop your non-perishable food items in our donation bins!

Donate 5 or more non-perishable items, at one of our Madison locations, and receive 20% off your entire order!*
